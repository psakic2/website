About the Building

A Note About Our Building

Oblivion sits on the top floor of a protected Old Town building — by heritage law, no elevator can be installed inside these walls. The climb is approximately 80 steps over four floors, worth knowing in advance.

The reward begins on the way up. Windows along the staircase open directly onto the ancient walls of Diocletian's Palace — by the time you reach your door, you've already glimpsed pieces of the Empire, and arrived at the quietest, most private corner of the city.

Our Services

✦ Breakfast at Restaurant Luxor
Served at the wonderful Restaurant Luxor, just 2 minutes' walk from our door. We provide breakfast vouchers — available as a paid service if not included in your booking. Ask us for options.
✦ Airport Shuttle
Convenient airport transfer with our trusted driver, picking you up at arrivals. €40 paid directly to the driver. Please book in advance.
