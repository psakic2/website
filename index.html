Welcome

A City Within a City

Most stays in Split are 'a short walk from the Palace.' Oblivion is something rarer — a building set so close that, from your window, you can almost reach out and touch a wall laid in A.D. 305. Three thousand people still live within those walls today. Their footsteps wear the same stone that Roman emperors once walked. This is the heart of the heart of Split.
